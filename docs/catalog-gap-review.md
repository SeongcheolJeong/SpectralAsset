# Catalog Gap Review

## Summary

The current generated baseline now exceeds the original `v1.0` count target through the first forty-eight `P3` coverage-expansion batches:

- `82` traffic signs
- `44` traffic lights

The remaining gaps are now mostly about deeper roadside-support context, later roadway breadth, and measurement quality rather than headline asset count.

## Key Findings

- `traffic_sign` is no longer limited to the original Vienna-core starter set: the baseline now includes heavier-weathered yield, no-entry, and construction panels plus hospital, parking, hotel, airport, bus-station, truck-route, bypass, centre, `CENTRO`, `CENTRUM`, `AEROPORTO`, `METRO`, `PORTO`, `FERRY`, `STAZIONE`, and `GARE` service panels, route-shields, stacked destination-guide signs, and overhead-guide variants, and the scenes place separate sign backs, brackets, and overhead-frame assemblies for representative sign families.
- `traffic_light` is no longer limited to the original five heads: the baseline now includes beacon-style, warning-flasher, compact and full-size lane-control, rail-crossing, transit-priority, compact and full-size bus/tram priority, compact bicycle and pedestrian-bicycle follow-up heads, transit-priority diamond follow-up heads, pedestrian-wait, school-warning, single/dual/quad-lunar preemption including a dual-lunar box follow-up, and dedicated directional-arrow or U-turn-arrow units, and the scenes place explicit backplates, mast-hanger context, truss and curved-mast cantilever frame/dropper context, brace/backspan/mount-plate hardware, side-mount brackets, rail-gate context, cabinet variants, and a junction box instead of implying all support context through poles alone.
- `road_surface` is no longer limited to the clean dry/wet urban starter set: the baseline now has patched, distressed, gravel-shoulder, unsealed-transition, construction-plate, milled-overlay, trench-cut, pothole, edge-dropoff, crowned rural-lane, dirt-track, bridge-joint, bridge-approach, lane-drop, barrier-taper, curb-bulbout, ramp-bridge-tie, ramp-gore, median-refuge-nose, roundabout truck-apron, splitter-island, outer-ring-edge, and bypass-slip-lane panels, bus-bay pullout, service-lane apron, curbside drop-off, alley-access, slip-lane pedestrian-island, mountable-apron corner, floating bus-stop island, transfer-platform, and separator-island composites, retaining-wall cut, shoulder-shelf, and abutment transitions, and deeper shoefly, left-hand contraflow, detour-staging, laydown-bay, or temporary-access work-zone composites that make the current scenes less schematic.
- `road_marking` is no longer minimal: the generator now ships directional arrows, edge-line color variants, worn crossing and stop-line variants, yellow centerline families, turn-pocket `ONLY` stencils, school-zone and school-bus queue markings, `SLOW` and `XING` legends, curbside arrows, bus/bike lane legends, hatched median/island panels, raised-marker variants, boxed curbside/transit legends, pick-up and taxi curbside boxes, red `NO PARK`, green `PERMIT`, white `VALET`, and green `EV ONLY` curbside-reservation boxes, white `WAIT` and `QUEUE` curbside-queue boxes, `DROP OFF` and `KISS RIDE` school-dropoff stencils, white/green separator-buffer panels, left/right separator-arrow and separator-chevron panels, curb-color segments, loading zigzag delimiters, and red/green conflict-zone surfacing that make the scenes less schematic.
- `road_furniture` now covers much more of the roadside-support tail because the latest batches added utility poles, smaller cabinet/junction variants, service disconnect and metering detail, pull boxes, pad-mount transformers, explicit sign/signal attachment hardware, transit-stop shelter/totem/bench detail, loading-zone curbside context, rail-gate mast/arm/bell/controller detail, queue-rail/shelter-panel/curb-ramp/clamp-hardware follow-up assets, truss and curved-mast cantilever signal-frame/dropper detail, shelter trash, route-map, lean-rail, curb-separator, hanger-clamp follow-up assets, the newer cantilever-anchor, footing-collar, single/quad-dropper, rail-power, rail-relay, validator-pedestal, and timetable-blade microsupport pieces, cantilever service ladders/platforms, a rail bungalow and battery box, queue-stanchion and boarding-guardrail curbside follow-up support, and now diagonal brace, backspan, and mount-plate cantilever follow-up pieces.
- with the latest signal loop now landed, the next obvious expansion target shifts from `traffic_light` toward another `road_furniture` pass for cantilever maintenance/support-tail, rail-control ancillary, and transit microsupport follow-up work.
- `traffic_light` still has room to grow, but the remaining gaps are now mostly about still narrower regional edge cases, smaller controller-face nuance, and measured SPD quality rather than missing starter head families.
- `traffic_sign` now has better assembly, locale, weathering, service-wayfinding, transfer-guide, route-shield, destination-stack, and overhead-guide depth, so its remaining gaps are mostly about still narrower multilingual/local-service breadth, larger gantry/fork-guide variation, and measured retroreflective behavior rather than missing starter hardware.

- Future count expansion should not break the naming and manifest contracts in [asset-spec.md](asset-spec.md).
- Coverage reviews should distinguish count completeness from fidelity completeness. The current pack is count-complete, but not depth-complete.
